## Strategist adoption (the non-technical leverage check)

The Q1 plan said "building the console is half the work; getting strategists to live in it daily is the other half." Answer these by asking strategists:

1. Did `/admin/console` become your daily home? If not, why?
2. What signal would you add to the row?
3. What signal could we drop?
4. Score formula working? (`overdue × 3 + bad reviews × 2 + token issues × 1`)
5. How many tabs do you have open right now vs at Q1 start?

If the honest answer to #1 is "no" — that's the most important finding of the quarter, not a side note.
